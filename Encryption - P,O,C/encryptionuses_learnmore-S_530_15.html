Encryption Uses

30.01.2018

Encryption is also used to protect data in transit, for example data being transferred via networks (e.g internet, e-commerce), mobile phones , wireless microphones, wireless intercom systems, Bluetooth devices and bank automatic teller machines. There Public-key encryption was first described in a secret document in 1973before then all encryption schemes were symmetric-key (also called private-key). There have been numerous reports of data in transit being intercepted in recent years. Data should also be encrypted when transmitted across networks in order to protect against eavesdropping of network traffic by unauthorized users.
